In April 2019, Vince Carter announced his interest in another year in the NBA. Mr. Carter is widely regarded as the best dunker the game has seen, but that's not the reason another season is so important for the 8 time All-Star. Another season would make a record breaking 22 seasons, the most ever by an NBA player.

PERFORMANCE & LONGEVITY

NBA players' contracts are commonly 9 figures long. It's an understatement to say such an investment is always a risky affair. A team would jump at the opportunity to figure the potential length of players' carrer prior to breaking the bank to sign them. This application uses historical data to find similar players and predict a selected player's likely longevity and success in the league.
